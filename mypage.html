    Why I Travel

    From 2019-2020, I studied at BYU-Hawaii. While I was there, I met students from all over the world. As I learned more about their culture and traditions, I started to have a desire to travel. I've had the opportunity to live in Costa Rica and Spain. I also was able to travel to Europe and visited countries such as France, Italy, England, and the Czech Reupblic. I am studying Experience Design at BYU and hope to continue to travel when I'm older.
